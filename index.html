Covid-19

Stay Home, Stay Safe

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 virus. Most people who fall sick with COVID-19 will experience mild to moderate symptoms and recover without special treatment. However, some will become seriously ill and require medical attention.

Steps to Protect yourself

Wear A Face Mask

Wearing mask it not only important ,it's life saving. You may be a carrier of COVID-19 and be asymptomatic. By wearing a mask you're stopping the spread and protecting others.

learn more

Wash Your Hands

Washing your hands is one of the simplest ways you can protect. Use soap and water or an alcohol-based hand rub. This will kill viruses that may be on your hands.

learn more

Avoid Close Contact

The CDC recommends maintaining a distance of approximately 6 feet from others in public places. This distance will help you avoid direct contact with respiratory droplets produced by coughing or sneezing.
